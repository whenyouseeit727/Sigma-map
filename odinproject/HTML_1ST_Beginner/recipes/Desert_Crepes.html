Desert Crepe


This crêpe recipe is essential for a fancy breakfast or eye-catching dessert. Sprinkle warm crêpes with sugar and lemon, or serve with whipped cream, ice cream, and fruit.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 ⅓ cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Gather all the ingredients
  2. Step 2
    Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Step 3
    Heat a medium skillet or crêpe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel.
  4. Step 4
    Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crêpe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface.
  5. Step 5
    Cook until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  6. Step 6
    Serve warm and enjoy!
